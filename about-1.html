Welcome to AL TAREEQ KITCHEN

Market leader in the field of commercial kitchen equipment

Established in 2014, AL TAREEQ Kitchen is your trusted partner in the catering industry, specializing in the expert installation, maintenance, cleaning, and servicing of extract and air supply systems. With years of invaluable experience, we have meticulously crafted a stellar reputation based on reliability, expert craftsmanship, and competitive pricing. Operating across the UAE, our dedicated team ensures seamless service delivery, promising 100% customer satisfaction. Our seasoned installation engineers are committed to excellence, guaranteeing minimal disruption to your business from the initial free onsite survey to the completion of the project. Choose AL TAREEQ Kitchen for unparalleled expertise and a commitment to quality that stands the test of time. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we are dedicated to enhancing your culinary business with our exceptional services.
